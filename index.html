Crop Disease Detection

Detecting crop diseases using machine learning

What is Crop Disease Detection?

Crop Disease Detection is a machine learning application that can identify diseases in crops using images of leaves. It uses a deep learning algorithm to analyze the images and detect signs of disease, such as discoloration, spots, or abnormal growth. This tool can help farmers quickly identify diseased crops and take action to prevent further spread of the disease.

How it works

The Crop Disease Detection model is trained using a large dataset of images of healthy and diseased crops. The model learns to recognize patterns in the images that are associated with specific diseases. When a new image is uploaded, the model compares it to its database and predicts the most likely disease based on the patterns it recognizes in the image.

Crops List

  • Tomato - a juicy and nutritious fruit used in many culinary dishes
    • Common diseases: Early Blight, Late Blight, Fusarium Wilt, Verticillium Wilt
  • Potato - a starchy root vegetable commonly used for fries, chips, and mashed potatoes
    • Common diseases: Late Blight, Early Blight, Potato Virus Y, Potato Cyst Nematodes
  • Maize - a cereal crop that is commonly used for food, animal feed, and fuel
    • Common diseases: Common Rust, Northern Leaf Blight, Southern Leaf Blight, Fusarium Ear Rot
  • Wheat - a cereal grain that is widely cultivated for its seeds and used to make flour and bread
    • Common diseases: Leaf Rust, Stripe Rust, Head Blight, Septoria Leaf Blotch
  • Rice - a staple food for more than half of the world's population
    • Common diseases: Rice Blast, Brown Spot, Sheath Blight, Bacterial Leaf Blight
  • Soybean - a legume that is a major source of protein and oil
    • Common diseases: Soybean Rust, Sudden Death Syndrome, Phytophthora Root Rot, White Mold
  • Cotton - a soft, fluffy fiber that is used to make clothing and textiles
    • Common diseases: Cotton Leaf Curl Virus, Verticillium Wilt, Fusarium Wilt, Boll Rot

Crop Diseases List

  • Tomato Early Blight - a fungal disease that affects tomato plants and causes leaf spots and defoliation
    • Symptoms: circular brown spots with concentric rings on the leaves, stems, and fruits; yellowing and defoliation
    • Management: crop rotation, sanitation, fungicides
  • Tomato Late Blight - a destructive disease that affects tomato and potato plants
    • Symptoms: dark lesions on leaves and stems; white moldy growth on the underside of leaves; rapid plant decline
    • Management: fungicides, crop rotation, removing infected plants and debris
  • Maize Common Rust - a fungal disease that affects maize plants and causes yellow to brownish-orange rust pustules on leaves
    • Symptoms: yellow to brownish-orange rust pustules on leaves; stunted growth and reduced yields
    • Management: crop rotation, resistant varieties, fungicides
  • Wheat Leaf Rust - a fungal disease that affects wheat plants and causes yellowish-orange pustules on leaves
    • Symptoms: yellowish-orange pustules on leaves; stunted growth and reduced yields
    • Management: crop rotation, resistant varieties, fungicides
  • Potato Late Blight - a devastating disease that caused the Irish Potato Famine
    • Symptoms: dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems; white moldy growth on the underside of leaves
    • Management: removing infected plants and debris, fungicides
  • Rice Blast - a fungal disease that affects rice plants and causes diamond-shaped lesions on leaves and neck blast on panicles
    • Symptoms: diamond-shaped lesions on leaves; neck blast on panicles; stunted growth and reduced yields
    • Management: resistant varieties, cultural practices, fungicides
  • Soybean Rust - a fungal disease that affects soybean plants and causes chlorotic and necrotic lesions on leaves and stems
    • Symptoms: chlorotic and necrotic lesions on leaves and stems; defoliation; reduced yields
    • Management: resistant varieties, fungicides, cultural practices
  • Cotton Leaf Curl Virus - a viral disease that affects cotton plants and causes curling of leaves and stunting of plant growth
    • Symptoms: curling of leaves; stunting of plant growth; reduced yields
    • Management: resistant varieties, insecticides to control vector insects
